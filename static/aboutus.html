Our Services

AGA is a consultant in these market segments

“AG Alchemy Consultancy Sdn Bhd (AGA)” is a Consultancy and Project Management Company established as an independent firm of consultants. AGA specializes in project cost estimation, engineering design & and drawings, planning, and contract management / administration services. Founded with more than 30 years of combined experience in Engineering, Project Management, and Operation Management,

AGA is a Malaysian based company, in Shah Alam with relevant regional experience covering the ASEAN, Saudi, India, Sudan, Spain and United Kingdom regions. The founders have successfully provided consulting and services to many clients covering Contractors, the Government Department, Engineering Consulting Firms, Small & Medium Industry companies, and several Multi-National Corporations. More than forty (40) projects and services covering engineering consultancy, project management, and advisory has been completed for various sectors such as Bridges, Road Construction & Slope Works, Gas Pipelines, Power Plants, Manufacturing Plants, Sewage Treatment, Sewage and water Facilities, Agricultural, Housing & Property and Downstream Oil & Gas. New industries include the Telecommunications sector
