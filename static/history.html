Our History

AGA specializes in design & engineering, engineering drawings, project cost estimation, planning, project management and contract management / administration services. Founded with more than 20 years of combined experience in Engineering, Project Management, and Operation Management, AGA a Malaysian based company, in Shah Alam with relevant local and regional experience covering the South East Asia, Middle East and India.

The founders have successfully provided consulting and services to many clients covering Contractors, Government Department, Engineering Consulting Firms, Small & Medium Industry companies and several Multi National Corporations. More than forty (40) projects and services covering engineering consultancy, project management and advisory has been completed for various sectors such as Bridges, Road Construction & Slope Works, Gas Pipelines, Power Plants, Manufacturing Plants, Sewage Treatment, Sewage & Water Facilities, Agricultural, Housing & Property and Downstream Oil & Gas. New industries include the Telecommunications sector.
